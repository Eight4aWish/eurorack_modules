Internal LPG & envelope

  1. Patch a gate to TRIG; leave LEVEL unpatched so Plaits uses its built-in LPG envelope.
  2. Decay: hold Plaits' first menu button and turn MORPH. Four yellow LEDs show the value.
  3. Response (VCFA ↔ VCA): hold the same button and turn TIMBRE.

⚠ When unpatching any CV with TRIG patched, reset that channel's attenuverter to 12 o'clock or the internal envelope will start modulating the parameter.
